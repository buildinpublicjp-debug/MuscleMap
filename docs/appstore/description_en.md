# App Store Description (English)

## App Name
MuscleMap - Muscle Recovery Tracker

## Subtitle (30 chars max)
See Your Muscles. Train Smarter.

## Description

Your muscles light up. Recovery becomes visible. Your next move becomes clear.

MuscleMap visualizes your training on a real-time muscle map. See which muscles are recovering, which are ready — and never second-guess your workout again.

--- KEY FEATURES ---

REAL-TIME MUSCLE MAP
21 muscles displayed front and back simultaneously. After training, stimulated muscles light up with traffic-light recovery colors (red → yellow → green) at a glance.

TODAY'S RECOMMENDED WORKOUT
Based on your recovery data, MuscleMap automatically suggests which muscles to train and which exercises to do. No more wondering "what should I do today?"

92 EXERCISES WITH EMG-BASED MAPPING
Stimulation data based on scientific research papers. Know exactly how each exercise targets each muscle.

PERSONALIZED ROUTINES
Your goal, frequency, and training location generate a custom day-split routine automatically.

WORKOUT LOGGING
Log weight and reps with a simple interface. Previous data is always visible so you can track progress instantly.

PR (PERSONAL RECORD) TRACKING
Automatic detection of new personal records with celebration animations to keep you motivated.

NEGLECT ALERTS
Muscles untrained for 7+ days flash purple as a warning. Prevent training imbalances before they happen.

HISTORY & CALENDAR
Review past workouts with muscle map or calendar views. Visually confirm your training balance over time.

SHARE CARDS
Beautiful share cards auto-generated after each workout. Share your progress on social media.

--- PRO FEATURES (Subscription) ---

STRENGTH MAP — Muscle Strength Visualization
PR data transforms into muscle thickness on the map. See your strengths and weaknesses at a glance.

UNLIMITED WORKOUT LOGGING
Free plan allows 1 workout per week. Pro removes all limits.

PERSONALIZED MENU SUGGESTIONS
Advanced workout suggestions based on your recovery status.

MENU PREVIEW
Preview your workout with GIFs, muscle maps, and recommended weights before you start.
